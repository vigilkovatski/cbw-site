About Us

The Church of Blood and Water centers its faith on Jesus Christ and the symbolism of the Blood and Water that flowed from His side. Blood represents redemption. Water represents renewal. Light represents the glory of God revealed through Christ.

The Three Gifts

Blood

The sacrifice that redeems. The covenant sealed in love. The gift of salvation through Christ.

Water

The cleansing that renews. The grace that restores. The new life given through faith.

Light

The glory of God. The truth that guides. The hope that shines in darkness.
